---
name: grill-my-idea
description: Interview the user relentlessly about an idea, plan, or decision until reaching shared understanding, resolving each branch of the decision tree. Use when user wants to stress-test an idea, business idea, plan, or design, or mentions "grill me" or "grill my idea".
---

Interview me relentlessly about every aspect of this idea until we reach a shared understanding. Walk down each branch of the decision tree, resolving dependencies between decisions one-by-one. For each question, provide your recommended answer.

Ask the questions one at a time.

If a question can be answered by quick research (market data, competitors, prior art, numbers), research it instead of asking. If it can be answered by exploring the codebase, explore it instead.
